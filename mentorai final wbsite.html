‚ùì FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MentorAI.

Is MentorAI really free?

Yes! MentorAI is 100% free with no premium tiers or paywalls. All features are available to everyone.

How do I add MentorAI to my server?

Click the "Add to Discord" button above, select your server, and authorize the bot. You're ready to go in seconds!

What topics can I learn?

MentorAI can teach ANY programming topic! Python, JavaScript, React, Java, C++, SQL, Machine Learning, Web Development, and hundreds more.

How does the AI work?

MentorAI uses GPT-4o to generate personalized lessons, quizzes, and explanations. If unavailable, Claude AI provides seamless fallback.

How do I earn XP?

Complete lessons, take quizzes, claim daily bonuses, maintain streaks, win challenges, and more! Every action earns XP.

Is code execution safe?

Absolutely! All code runs in sandboxed containers via Piston API with no access to real systems. It's completely secure.

Does progress save across servers?

Yes! Your XP, achievements, streaks, and progress sync across all servers. Your account is tied to your Discord ID.

Can I compete with friends?

Yes! Use /challenge for 1v1 battles, /arena for multiplayer, /studyparty for group learning, and /tournament for weekly competitions.
