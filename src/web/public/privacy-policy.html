Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 29, 2025

1. Introduction

MentorAI ("we", "our", or "the Bot") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Discord bot.

Our Commitment: We only collect data necessary to provide our educational services. We do not sell your data to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Data

Data Type Purpose Retention
Discord User ID Account identification & progress tracking Until account deletion
Discord Username Display on leaderboards Updated on each interaction
Server ID Server-specific settings Until bot removal from server

2.2 Learning Data

Data Type Purpose
Quiz scores & answers Track progress, personalize learning
XP & Level Gamification, leaderboards
Achievements Recognition, motivation
Learning streaks Encourage consistent learning
Completed lessons Track curriculum progress
Topic preferences Personalized recommendations

2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Message content (except direct commands to the Bot)
  • Personal information (name, email, address)
  • Financial information
  • Voice or video data
  • Data from other Discord bots or applications

3. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data exclusively to:

  • Provide personalized learning experiences
  • Track your progress and achievements
  • Display leaderboards and statistics
  • Improve our AI-generated content
  • Maintain and improve the Bot's functionality
  • Prevent abuse and enforce our Terms of Service

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • MongoDB Atlas - Encrypted database storage
  • Railway - Secure cloud hosting
  • HTTPS - All data transmission is encrypted

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties.

We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • With service providers (hosting, database) necessary to operate the Bot
  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or prevent fraud

6. Third-Party Services

MentorAI uses the following third-party services:

  • Discord API - To interact with Discord
  • OpenAI API - To generate educational content (no personal data shared)
  • MongoDB Atlas - Database storage

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their data practices.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of your stored data
  • Correction - Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion - Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out - Stop using the Bot at any time

To exercise these rights, use the /feedback command or contact us through our support server.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as you use the Bot. If you wish to delete your data:

  • Contact us via /feedback command
  • Your data will be deleted within 30 days
  • Some anonymized aggregate data may be retained for analytics

9. Children's Privacy

MentorAI is not intended for users under 13 years of age, in compliance with Discord's Terms of Service and COPPA regulations. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be announced through the Bot or our support server. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns:

  • Use the /feedback command in Discord
  • Join our support server
